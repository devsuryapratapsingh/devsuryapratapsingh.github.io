A little about how I build.

I build and ship cross-platform (Flutter) and native (Kotlin/Jetpack Compose, iOS) apps used by 10,000+ end users. I've achieved 99.5%+ crash-free sessions, cut API load times by 30% through smart caching, and shortened release cycles by 20% by building reusable component libraries.

I'm hands-on with Bloc/Cubit, Provider, and Riverpod on Flutter, plus MVVM with ViewModel + StateFlow on native Android — all wired to Clean Architecture so the next developer (often future-me) can actually maintain it. I've also shipped end-to-end payment integrations (Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal) with zero payment-related incidents.

Outside of shipping features, I work closely with design, backend, and product teams to turn technical constraints into realistic timelines — and I've mentored junior developers through pair-programming and code reviews.
