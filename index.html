About

How I build software teams can maintain.

I design and ship Flutter and native Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose) applications with clear module boundaries, testable domain logic, and release discipline. My day-to-day work covers feature delivery, crash triage, offline caching, payment flows, maps, and store publishing.

On Flutter I use Bloc/Cubit, Provider, and Riverpod. On Android I use ViewModel + StateFlow under MVVM. Both stacks sit on Clean Architecture so UI, domain, and data responsibilities stay separated as the product grows.

I partner with design, backend, and product on realistic scope, and I mentor juniors through reviews and pair programming rather than drive-by feedback.
