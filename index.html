A little about how I build.

I specialize in Flutter & Dart, with deep experience across state management (BLoC, Riverpod, Provider, GetX), Clean Architecture, and native platform integration via method channels.

I care about apps that don't just work — they perform well, handle edge cases gracefully, and are built on a codebase the next developer can actually maintain. I've shipped apps end-to-end: from architecture decisions to CI/CD pipelines to App Store/Play Store releases.

Outside of shipping features, I enjoy profiling jank out of janky ListViews, writing tests nobody asked for, and occasionally arguing about whether GetX is really that bad.
